Our Philosophy

Cooked with
memory and fire

Dukex was born from a simple belief: that the richness of African cuisine deserves a stage worthy of its complexity and beauty.

Head Chef Akinyi Otieno draws on traditions spanning from Morocco to Cape Town — slow-smoked meats, fermented grains, ancient spice routes — and elevates them with technique and intention.

Every plate tells a story. Every meal is a journey.
